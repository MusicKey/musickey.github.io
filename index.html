About

A Music Key Piano studio we are all about making piano an accessable and engaging instrument to play for all ages. Our instructors have entensive experience in piano and strive to make learning an enjoyable and engaging experience for students. Our goal is to teach students how to interperte and understand the music they are playing, motivate them to by introducing new musical possibilities and cater the learning experience to each students learning style.
