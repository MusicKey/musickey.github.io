Who We Are

Since 2007, Music Key has strived to provide students with the skills they need to play the piano. Our staff have extensive experience with piano, both on the education side and ther performance and competition side. Together we strive to make piano and engaging, enjoyanble and exciting instrument for our students to play for a lifetime.

Our Goals

We teach students how to learn and understand the various elements of music


We motivate students by introducing them to new musical possibilities and empowering them.

We understand our student's needs and tailor the learning experience to the students learning style

Our Principles

We believe effort is more important than the results.


We believe that learning piano should be a satisfying experience
