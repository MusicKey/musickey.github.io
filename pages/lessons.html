Lessons

Music Key piano studio offers private lessons in the Atlanta area and has few vacancies for new students. Music Key accepts students of all levels, we offers tutoring for high school, college and university students, audition preperation as well as participation in concerts and recitals.


About Our Lessons
  1. Lessons are typically one hour long however longer of shorter lessons can be scheduled depending on a students needs

  2. All lessons are done on a one-on-one basis however group of semiprivate lessons can be arranged.

  3. An assesment lesson is highly recommended for new players. Assesment lessons run thirty minutes and include an introducion to music, assesment of the students' skills and interests and assesment of the students overall musical ability.
